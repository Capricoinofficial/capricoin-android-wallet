Have your Peercoins always with you, in your pocket! You pay by quickly scanning a QR-code. As a merchant, you receive payments reliably and instantly. Peercoin Wallet is the first mobile Peercoin app, and arguably also the most secure!

FEATURES:

• No registration, web service or cloud needed! This wallet is de-centralized and peer to peer.
• Display of Peercoin amount in PPC, mPPC and µPPC.
• Conversion to and from national currencies.
• Sending and receiving of Peercoin via NFC, QR-codes or Peercoin URLs.
• Address book for regularly used Peercoin addresses.
• When you're offline, you can still pay via Bluetooth.
• System notification for received coins.
• App widget for Peercoin balance.

You can find more about Peercoin at
http://peercoin.net

If you want to contribute to Peercoin Wallet, the project is located at
https://github.com/MatthewLM/peercoin-android-wallet
